Introduction

In 2017, between February 23rd and March 9th, I organized and taught a JavaFX course at my Bachelor's faculty.

The course was completely free and aimed at any other students on the faculty who were interested on learning about JavaFX. My motivation was that I believed my Bachelor's programme did not put enough emphasis on teaching students about GUI programming.

The main objective of the course was to teach students to develop a very basic JavaFX application, thus providing them with a starting point from which to start to learn how to use that technology.
