Introduction

VeaR is a short first-person horror Virtual Reality (VR) demo experience developed in Unreal Engine 4 where players fight mentally against their own paranoia through a TV that is broadcasting themselves trapped in a square-shaped room. Furthermore, they can interact with the TV by changing its properties by using a panel of buttons located in front of them.

Development

This demo has been developed in 4 days for educational purposes, as a part of one of my Master's courses.
