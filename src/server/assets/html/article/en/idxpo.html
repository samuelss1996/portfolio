Introduction

The Interaction Design Exhibition (IDXPO) is an event hold at the Universeum science center yearly.

A total of 12 different projects by around 60 students under the theme of Showing the Invisible were created and showcased during the IDXPO 2019 25th October and 26th October.

My group developed Nuclear Panic, a 2D endless runner scrolling platformer video game.
