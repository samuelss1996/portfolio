Introduction

Nuclear Panic is a 2D infinite-runner side-scroller platformer video game developed for the Interaction Design and Game Design Exhibition (IDXPO) 2019.

In Nuclear Panic, players take control of a worker at a nuclear plant where a nuclear meltdown just occurred, which produced radioactive emissions. The character flees from this powerful and lethal source of radiation (Alpha radiation), while players have to avoid obstacles and hazardous elements, such as other kinds of radiation (Beta and Gamma), by jumping or deciding not to jump, depending on each case.
